Fields of Interests : Mathematics, Applications of Mathematics
Research Interests :
  1. Mathematical and numerical analysis of second order linear partial differential equations
  2. Asymptotic analysis of the solution of second order linear partial differential equations in non-smooth domains
  3. Spectral analysis of second order linear partial differential equations
  4. The h-, p- and hp-versions of the finite element methods
  5. Domain decomposition methods
  6. Software development and computer simulations
Current Research Projects
  1. Adaptive h-, p- and hp-versions of the finite element method for Maxwell's equations in polygonal and polyhedral domains.
  2. Adaptive h-, p- and hp-versions of the finite element method for parabolic evolution problems in polygonal and polyhedral domains.
  3. Adaptive h-, p- and hp-versions of the finite element method for hyperbolic evolution problems in polygonal and polyhedral domains.
