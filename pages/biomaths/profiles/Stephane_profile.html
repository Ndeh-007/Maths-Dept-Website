Research Summary

Our research focus is the study of the geometrical, topological and algebraic structures of the group of diffeomorphisms of a compact oriented manifold (e.g. manifold with symplectic, contact, and cosymplectic structures) . This includes some aspects of topological dynamical systems. Our methods come from the theory of global analysis (analysis on manifolds).
