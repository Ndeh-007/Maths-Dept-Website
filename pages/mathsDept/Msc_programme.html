Our Programmes
Ranging from Bachelors through Masters to Ph.D, the different programs are structured to guide the student to in their studies.
Objectives
To initiate students to do studies and research in Pure and Applied Mathematics
Admission Requirements

In addition to University and Faculty requirements, applicants for an MSc degree programme should have an Honours Bachelor's degree in at least the Second-class division, or its equivalent, in the subject for which application is made.

Graduation Requirements

The student is required to earn at least 120 credits to be able to graduate with a Master degree in Mathematics. The 120 credits are distributed as follows:

  1. Compulsory university credits = 6
  2. Compulsory departmental credits = 72
  3. Credits for thesis project = 42
Skills to be Acquired

At the end of the programme the student is expected to be able to work with some independence in some specific area in mathematics.

Employment Opportunities

Graduates can seek for employment in secondary education and institutions where the teaching of Mathematics is required. Graduates are endowed with sufficient skills to be able for employment as Statisticians, Consultants, Management and Banking Officials. And in areas of information technology. Exceptionally brilliant students can be employed as junior research assistants in the Universities and other research institutes.
