About The Mathematical Biology Unit

The Mathematical and Computer Assisted Modelling Unit is a specialized Teaching and Research Unit of the University of Buea placed under the Administrative supervision of the Faculty of Science.

Objectives

As a specialized unit of the University of Buea, the Applied Mathematical and Computer Assisted Modelling Unit shall contribute to the attainment of the triple mission of teaching, research and outreach assigned to the University by Government. The specific objectives of the Applied Mathematical and Computer Assisted Modelling Unit shall be the following:

  1. To carry out Applied mathematical modelling research of high quality for the development of the nation.
  2. To collaborate with relevant establishments and departments of the University of Buea in the training of applied mathematicians and in the applications of mathematics in scientific programs that require knowledge of mathematical modelling and computer simulation.
  3. To serve as a hub for exchange of research and learning between the university and mathematics alumni who are established scientists.
  4. To organise short-courses, seminars, conferences and programmes for the promotion of the applications of mathematics to inform our communities, and by extension, the Central African Region on development, health and related problems.
  5. To serve as a data collection point for health information dashboards to inform on related health issues.
  6. Offer a platform for computer assisted modelling and development and use of applications of Artificial intelligence (AI) platforms.
  7. To generate income for the University through provision of consultancy and analytical services, development and marketing of mathematical and statistical software products.
